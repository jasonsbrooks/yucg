Who Are We?

Yale Undergraduate Consulting (YUC) is an undergraduate-run consulting group offering strategy and marketing consulting services to local, international and multinational clients. YUC offers the full range of consulting services, and emphasizes a hands-on approach to developing applicable strategies and, when possible, implementing them directly. YUC is organized into two branches, Strategy and Marketing.

Students work in teams of four or five students led by a Project Manager who acts as the primary client liaison and ensures the team provides only the highest quality product. Teams are designed to include a balance of experienced upperclassmen as well as new-joiners, and they will often include students who have interned with consulting or financial firms. The recruitment process is a careful, highly selective process modeled after the best practices of professional management consulting firms so our clients can be confident that only the most dedicated and effective students are on each engagement.

Strategy

The Strategy branch provides innovative solutions to guide business development, restructuring and growth. Within Strategy, we offer traditional management consulting services as well as website and application-focused services in a specialized Strategy team, the Project Development Group.

Our traditional services often focus on market entry and growth strategy, although we offer the full swathe of strategic services. Students are trained to adapt quickly to each assignment and we encourage innovation. We frequently apply hands-on techniques such as field research, focus groups and specialized surveying so the conclusions we draw accurately reflect the present market conditions and customer base.

The Project Development Group (PDG) combines strategic thinking and implementation. The PDG works with clients to both develop and execute prototypes for applications and websites. PDG teams include several programming-focused members as well as consultants so that clients can see the concept take shape and have a starting point for further product development.

Previous clients Include: PointWC, Yale Insignia, Proximiant, The Yale Entrepreneurial Institute, TheHotlist.com

Marketing

The Marketing branch applies fresh thinking and up-to-the minute methodologies to develop effective marketing strategies for our clients. For scalable, high-impact and low-cost marketing campaigns we emphasize use of online portals and networks. Our unparalleled insight into optimization of user experience and youth trends allows our clients to reach their Gen Y customers on a very real level.

Previous clients include: Kaplan, Teach For America, HavetoHave.com, TheHotlist.com, CampusFood.com, Pfizer Inc.

History

YUC was initially founded under the name Maya Consulting and Marketing. Recently, we changed our name and reincorporated our organization with the Yale administration to better express our mission as the premiere consulting group at Yale.

Our Philosophy

We believe that the best way to approach strategic problems is to break them down into component parts. Our approach is heavily data driven and analytical, applying both quantitative and qualitative tools to understand the problem at hand. We believe in finding creative, often overlooked solutions to these problems through discussion and brainstorming.

Meet our Team
Sara Elenowitz

President

An Economics major at Yale College, Sara has been a member of Yale Undergraduate Consulting (YUC) since 2009. Starting in the Strategy group, she spent three semesters as an Engagement Manager for Strategy and Marketing, a semester as External Vice President and is entering her second semester as President of YUC.
